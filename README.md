# Automated-VSA-Pre-Market-Stock-Scanner
# The scripts scans PreMarket NASDAQ stocks that are in the top gainers list following the 4am (US) PreMarket open that have dropped at least 15% from its highest point presenting a potential opportunity for a reversal where you buy the stock towards the lowest point of day where it find support and there is seller exhaustion. You then sell when the stock climbs back up to its resistance (high point) or when your profit targets are met (5%,10%,etc)

The results are sent to your discord server so you can be notified on the go.
